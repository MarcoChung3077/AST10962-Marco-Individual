Before we begin, I want to introduce my idol Stephen Curry - the man who ignited my passion for basketball and taught me to grow through adversity. When coaches said I was "too small," I adopted Curry's mindset - using perceived weaknesses as fuel. Now as team captain, I lead not just with skills honed through thousands of practice hours, but with Curry's signature joy for the game. His example taught me that excellence isn't about never failing, but about rising each time you fall - a philosophy that's made me both a better player and person.
